Description of Service

Purvale is a privacy-focused personal finance application that allows you to:

  • Record income and expense transactions
  • Manage multiple accounts and custom categories
  • Set budgets and view financial statistics
  • Track a private People ledger for money between friends
  • Create encrypted local backups and export data in CSV format
  • Optionally secure the App with biometric authentication

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not provide cloud storage, synchronization, or remote backup services.

Free and Pro Features

Purvale is free to download with optional Pro features unlocked through a one-time in-app purchase.
