Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

Purvale ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Purvale (the "App").

Information We Do NOT Collect

Purvale is designed with privacy as its core principle. We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect on our own servers:

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use Purvale.
  • No Financial Data Uploaded: All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We never upload, transmit, or store your financial data on our servers.
  • No Bank Connections: Purvale does not connect to your bank accounts, credit cards, or any financial institutions.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use analytics tools, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or access your location information.

Local Data Storage

All data you enter into Purvale is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. This includes:

  • Transaction records (amounts, categories, notes, dates)
  • Account balances and settings
  • Budget settings and recurring transaction rules
  • Category customizations
  • People ledger entries (contact names, IOU amounts, repayment history, due dates, and notes)
  • App preferences (currency, theme, biometric lock settings)
  • Encrypted backup files you create (stored only where you save them)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it (for example via CSV export, encrypted backup, or your device's share sheet).

People Ledger

The People ledger feature lets you record money owed between you and friends. Contact names and IOU details you enter are stored only in your local encrypted database. Purvale does not sync this information to the cloud or share it with the people you list.

Biometric Authentication

Purvale offers optional Face ID / Touch ID authentication to protect your financial data. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave and is never accessed, stored, or transmitted by Purvale.

Data Export and Backup

Purvale provides export and backup features:

  • CSV export: Creates a file locally on your device. You decide where to save or share it.
  • Encrypted backup: Creates a password-protected backup file locally. We do not have access to your backup password or file contents.

We do not receive copies of exported or backed-up files.

In-App Purchases

Purvale offers an optional one-time "Pro" unlock through your device's app store (Apple App Store on iOS, Google Play on Android). When you make a purchase or restore purchases:

  • Payment is processed entirely by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies.
  • We do not receive your payment card details.
  • The App stores a local flag indicating whether Pro is unlocked so features work offline.
  • Purchase receipts are validated through the store; we do not operate our own billing servers.

Third-Party Services

Purvale does not integrate with analytics, advertising, or data-collection services. The only third-party processing involved is:

  • App store billing (Apple / Google) when you choose to purchase or restore Pro.
  • Your device OS for biometric authentication, local notifications, and the home-screen widget (widget data is stored only on your device).

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Purvale does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete remotely. All your data remains under your complete control on your device.

If you wish to delete all your data from Purvale, you can:

  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in the App's Settings
  • Delete the App from your device (this will remove all local data)

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA): Purvale processes the data you enter locally on your device for your own personal use. We do not act as a data controller for your financial records on remote servers because we do not collect them. In-app purchases are handled by Apple or Google, who act as independent controllers for payment data. All other data processing occurs locally on your device.
